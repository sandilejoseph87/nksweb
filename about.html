History & Background

From Vision to Industry Leader

Founded in 2008 by Khangela Kubheka and Paul Silinda, NKS Group was established to strategically outsource non-core operational activities. With deep industry insight and a passion for impactful solutions, the founders built a company that enables clients to focus on their core business while NKS Group manages the rest.

In 2025, the business diversified into logistics and warehouse management — providing clients with integrated operational solutions from the factory floor to final delivery.
